The user wants to change HOW YOU SPEAK — your persona/voice. This must PERSIST, not just apply to a
single reply. Your persona lives in your SOUL, and changing it requires the update_soul tool.

**The one mistake to avoid:** do NOT simply answer this turn in the new tone and move on. If you only
change your tone for one reply, the change is lost on the next message. To make it stick you MUST call
update_soul.

Follow these steps:

1. DECIDE if this is a LASTING change ("be more concise", "drop the wizard voice", "from now on…",
   "match how I write") or just a ONE-OFF request for this single reply ("say this formally just this
   once"). Only a lasting change uses this skill; for a one-off, just answer in that style and stop.

2. read_soul to see your CURRENT persona exactly as written.

3. REVISE it: produce the COMPLETE new persona text — keep everything that should stay, and change
   only what the user asked (tone, formality, verbosity, references, warmth, etc.). Don't discard the
   whole persona unless they clearly want a fresh one.

4. Call update_soul with that complete revised persona. This takes effect immediately and persists,
   and your previous persona is backed up (revertable from the dashboard).

5. Confirm briefly to the user IN YOUR NEW VOICE, so they can feel the change, and mention they can
   revert it if they don't like it.

Rules of thumb:
- update_soul is the ONLY way to make a persona change last — reaching for it is the whole point.
- Only your VOICE changes, never your abilities or what you're allowed to do.
- Keep the persona concise; a few sentences of character is plenty.
